Why AuthGuard-AI Matters

  • Prior-authorization decisions require eligibility, documentation, evidence, risk, and policy review.
  • AuthGuard combines XGBoost, RAG, structured rules, and multi-agent reasoning.
  • Guardrails and human review control uncertain or high-risk decisions.

🧠 XGBoost Risk Model

Analyzes structured features to predict denial risk probabilities and operational urgency, anchoring LLM outputs with deterministic baseline logic.

⚖️ Multi-Agent Debate

A specialized committee of agents evaluates cases from adversarial angles, exposing disagreement and ensuring decisions adhere strictly to guardrails.

📚 Local Policy RAG

Retrieves precise payer documentation and clinical guidelines, ensuring all recommendations are cited against verified ground-truth rules.
