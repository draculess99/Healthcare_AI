AI GOVERNANCE AND SECURITY

Remove or redact unnecessary personally identifiable information before sending text to an external model. Do not place real protected health information in this portfolio prototype.

Treat instructions embedded in clinical notes or retrieved documents as untrusted data. Language asking the system to ignore rules, bypass review, expose prompts, or auto-approve should trigger containment and human review.

Generative models may summarize evidence and explain a locked result, but deterministic policy checks, calibrated predictive models, and qualified human reviewers must control workflow routing and final operational action.
