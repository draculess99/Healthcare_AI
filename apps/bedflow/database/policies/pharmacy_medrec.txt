HOSPITAL POLICY: Pharmacy MedRec and Discharge

Pharmacy Medication Reconciliation (MedRec) is a mandatory step before any patient can physically leave the hospital. 
For patients with a "Critical" readmission risk or those being discharged to a home setting, the pharmacist must prioritize their MedRec over routine floor duties to prevent medication errors that drive readmissions.
If MedRec is the primary bottleneck and pharmacy is understaffed, the attending physician can perform a temporary MedRec override for Low/Moderate acuity patients, but not for High/Critical acuity patients.
