HOSPITAL POLICY: SNF and Rehab Transfers

All patients requiring discharge to a Skilled Nursing Facility (SNF) or Inpatient Rehab must have their physical therapy (PT) and occupational therapy (OT) evaluations completed prior to case management finalizing the transfer packet. 
If the patient's acuity level is High or Critical, they must hold discharge until the receiving facility explicitly confirms they have the staffing ratios to support the patient's specific diagnosis.
For patients with a "High" readmission risk, the transferring physician must conduct a warm handoff call with the accepting physician at the SNF.
